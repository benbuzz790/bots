## Risks and Mitigation
**Risk:** Complex parameter types may be difficult to collect interactively
**Mitigation:** Implement template-based systems and provide good defaults
**Risk:** User experience may become overwhelming with too many options
**Mitigation:** Implement progressive disclosure and smart defaults
**Risk:** Performance may degrade with complex parameter collection
**Mitigation:** Implement lazy loading and caching where appropriate
**Risk:** Backward compatibility may be broken
**Mitigation:** Maintain existing interfaces and add new functionality incrementally
